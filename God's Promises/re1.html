Honor God

And this hope will not lead to dissappointment.
For we know how dearly God loves us,
because he has given us the Holy Spirit to fill our hearts with his love.Romans 5:5 NLT

What does placing your hope in God meant to you?

Knowing that he is always there for me. He gives us signs every day and they may not be the most subtle ones but they are there.
I know deep down in my heart is he speaking to me in a way that is guiding me in the direction that I need to go.
As the verse said he has given us the Holy Spirit to fill our hearts with his love. That also means we must trust Him and know he is there.
